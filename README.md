## Air Duct Cleaning in Almonte — What You Need to Know

Almonte's winters mean your furnace runs hard from November through 
March. Five months of continuous operation pushing air through 
ductwork that collects dust, pet dander, mold spores, and debris 
with every cycle.

Most homeowners never think about what's inside their ducts until 
something goes wrong.

---

## Warning Signs Your Ducts Need Cleaning

- Dust reappears within days of wiping surfaces
- Musty smell when the furnace first turns on
- Allergy symptoms worse indoors than outside
- Certain rooms always feel stuffy
- Energy bills slowly creeping up

Clogged ducts force your furnace to work harder — longer run times, 
higher energy costs, more wear on your equipment.

---

## What Professional Cleaning Involves

A proper service uses industrial negative-pressure vacuums and 
rotary brushes to clean the full duct network — not just the 
visible registers.

Key requirements:
- NADCA certification
- Industrial equipment (not household vacuums)
- Camera inspection before & after
- 2-3 hours minimum (not 30 minutes)
- Flat-rate pricing upfront
